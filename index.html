COMPARISON

Drone Patrols vs Traditional Security

A clear comparison of how aerial patrols outperform ground‑based security methods across large commercial and industrial sites.

Feature Drone Patrols Traditional Security
Visibility Full aerial visibility, thermal imaging, significantly reduced blindspots Limited to ground level, fixed camera angles
Response Time Rapid aerial deployment and fast site assessment Slow movement across large sites
Evidence Quality HD video, thermal footage, GPS‑tagged logs Variable quality, limited angles, often reactive
Deterrence High — visible aerial presence and lighting Moderate — predictable patrol routes
Coverage Speed Large areas can often be surveyed in minutes Slow, manual, labour‑dependent
Cost Efficiency High — multiple patrols per night at low cost Lower — expensive manpower and long patrol times
Night Capability Thermal imaging, low‑light HD cameras Limited visibility, torch‑based searching
