THE SECURITY CHALLENGE

Traditional Security Has Blind Spots

Construction sites, industrial estates and logistics facilities often rely on static CCTV systems and occasional patrols. While effective in certain areas, they can leave significant gaps in visibility across larger sites.

Traditional Security

  • Fixed camera positions create blind spots
  • Large sites can take significant time to patrol
  • Limited visibility behind buildings and structures
  • Incidents may only be discovered after they occur
  • Difficult to rapidly assess large areas

LB Drones Security Patrols

  • Rapid aerial visibility across large sites
  • Flexible patrol routes tailored to your site
  • Thermal capability during darkness
  • Recorded evidential footage
  • Enhanced awareness of developing situations

A Complement To Existing Security

LB Drones is designed to enhance existing security measures, not replace them. Our patrols provide an additional layer of visibility that can support security teams, CCTV systems and site management.

HOW IT WORKS

A Structured Security Process

Every deployment begins with planning and an agreed patrol route tailored to the client’s site.

01

Site Assessment

A daytime survey is conducted to understand the layout and vulnerabilities.

02

Patrol Design

A bespoke patrol route is designed and agreed with site management.

03

Deployment

Patrols are conducted at agreed dates and times.

04

Monitoring

Any unusual activity is observed, recorded and assessed.

05

Reporting

Footage and optional reporting packages are delivered after the patrol.

INCIDENT MONITORING

When Visibility Matters Most

If suspicious activity is identified during a patrol, the objective is simple: maintain observation, document events and provide timely information to authorised stakeholders.

01

Detect

Suspicious activity is identified through visual and thermal observation.

02

Observe

The situation is monitored and recorded to establish a clear understanding of events.

03

Notify

Relevant stakeholders can be informed where appropriate.

04

Support

Information can be relayed to support informed decision-making.

05

Report

Recorded footage and observations can be provided following the incident.
